The 4I Model

AI removes the grind. Human value moves up. The book maps the terrain where your contribution still matters — four dimensions that machines can assist but never replace.

Interpretive You turn raw information into judgment. Where AI produces output, you provide meaning — reading context, spotting what matters, and making the call that data alone cannot.
Integrative You connect people, systems, and disciplines. The hardest problems cross boundaries that specialization created. You see the whole, synthesize across domains, and build bridges others can't.
Interpersonal You build trust when the room is uncertain. Relationships, influence, and the ability to move people toward shared goals — these compound over a career in ways no algorithm can replicate.
Imaginative You ask what future this work creates. Vision, originality, and the ability to generate genuinely new ideas remain distinctly human — and increasingly, the scarce input that AI scales everything else around.
